# Project Vision

## Vision Statement

ExamMaster is a modern educational platform designed to help students preparing for WAEC, JAMB, NECO, and GCE examinations achieve academic success through structured learning resources, realistic CBT practice, performance analytics, and personalized exam preparation tools.

## Mission

To provide students with a centralized, user-friendly, and scalable platform where they can study, practice examination questions, monitor their progress, and improve their performance.

## Long-Term Goal

To become one of the leading examination preparation platforms in Africa by providing high-quality learning resources, advanced CBT simulations, and data-driven learning insights.
