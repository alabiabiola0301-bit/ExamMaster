# Problem Statement

## Background

Students preparing for WAEC, JAMB, NECO, and GCE examinations often encounter challenges accessing quality educational resources and effective examination preparation tools.

## Current Challenges

- Study materials are scattered across multiple platforms.
- Many platforms do not provide realistic CBT practice.
- Students receive limited feedback on their performance.
- Tracking academic progress is difficult.
- Access to organized past questions and learning resources is often limited.

## Impact

These challenges reduce the effectiveness of exam preparation and make it difficult for students to identify their strengths and weaknesses before examinations.

## Proposed Solution

ExamMaster will provide a centralized platform that combines study resources, CBT practice, performance analytics, and progress tracking to help students prepare more effectively and confidently for their examinations.
