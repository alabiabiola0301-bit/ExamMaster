# Target Audience

## Primary Audience

### WAEC Candidates

Secondary school students preparing for the West African Senior School Certificate Examination (WASSCE).

### JAMB Candidates

Students preparing for the Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination (UTME) for admission into higher institutions.

### NECO Candidates

Students preparing for the National Examinations Council (NECO) examinations.

### GCE Candidates

Private candidates preparing for the General Certificate Examination (GCE).

---

## Secondary Audience

### Teachers and Tutors

Educators who assist students in preparing for examinations and may use the platform as a teaching resource.

### Parents and Guardians

Individuals interested in supporting and monitoring students' academic progress.

### Educational Institutions

Schools, tutorial centers, and coaching organizations that may adopt the platform for student training and CBT practice.

---

## User Needs

### Students Need

- Quality study materials
- CBT practice tests
- Performance tracking
- Exam preparation guidance

### Teachers Need

- Student performance monitoring
- Access to educational resources

### Institutions Need

- A scalable learning and examination preparation solution
